FEATURES

Everything your body deserves to know.

Aarohi is packed with powerful, beautiful features designed to empower you at every stage of life.

💬

Aarohi AI Companion

Your empathetic, always-available AI wellness companion — powered by on-device Apple Intelligence. Ask anything about your body, cycle, moods, or symptoms — Aarohi responds with medically-informed, judgement-free guidance. All conversations stay private on your device.

🏠

MySpace Hub

Your personalized daily dashboard. See your current life phase, track weekly body insights with AI-generated summaries, discover interactive stories and daily challenges, and keep an eye on health conditions to watch for — all in one beautiful place.

🌸

Life Phases Guide

Navigate all 6 life stages with confidence — Pre-Puberty, Puberty, Reproductive, Perimenopause, Menopause, and Post-Menopause. Each phase includes detailed hormone info, common concerns, care tips, and an interactive Phase Simulator with organ visualizations.

🔮

Reveal

Uncover the truth about your body. Scratch away the "Myth of the Day", navigate "Real Life Scenarios" to see what you would do, decode your symptoms, and finally answer that burning question: "Is It Normal?"

HOW IT WORKS

Your journey in 3 simple steps.

01

Choose Your Life Phase

During onboarding, enter your name and age. Aarohi auto-suggests your life phase and instantly tailors everything — content, stories, Body Map insights, and AI responses — to match your exact stage.

02

Explore & Track

Tap glowing zones on the Body Map, read interactive stories, scratch away myths, take on daily challenges, and log your symptoms — mood, energy, pain, and more. Learning about your body has never been this fun.

03

Get Personalized Insights

After tracking for a few days, Aarohi generates beautiful weekly insights highlighting patterns in your health, mood, and body. Ask Aarohi AI anything along the way.
